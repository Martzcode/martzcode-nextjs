Welcome to my first article! This file serves as an example to validate the
rendering pipeline: frontmatter validated with zod, MDX body, automatically
generated table of contents and reading time.

## Why this setup

Content is stored as `.mdx` files in `src/content/blog`. Each article exposes
its metadata in frontmatter and its body in Markdown enriched with JSX.

### Benefits

- No database required
- Content versioned in Git
- Editable in any editor

This demo article confirms that the reading page works. Feel free to duplicate
